Match Rules:

  • 8-Team Round Robin Tournament followed by playoffs with the finals played at GameSync.
  • During Round Robin, each match will consist of 2 maps played.
  • Before the match, there will be a captains meeting to determine which maps will be played.
  • Each team bans two maps and then each picks a map to play.
  • Matches start at 5pm and 7pm and are played on Monday or Wednesday nights.
  • Pausing is only available for severe technical issues.
  • 1 timeout available per team per map (60 seconds).

Match Structure:

  • At least one match each night will be streamed and casted.
  • It is expected that everyone plays in a meaningful and sportsmanlike manner.
